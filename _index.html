Welcome!

Seale and Sands Bowls club offers flat green bowling on a well kept 6 rink green in the beautiful Surrey countryside. Founded in 1928 the club has a friendly atmosphere and we welcome new members of all ages, beginners or seasoned bowlers.

Free taster session Sunday 14th May at 10am.
Click here for more information.

During the bowling season (middle of April to the middle of September) we play approximately 50 friendlies with local clubs and provide some of the possibly best teas in the county!

We are active in a number of local leagues and competitions including The Aldershot & District, West Surrey Bowling Association and Farnham Town. For a summary of our latest results see here.

On Tuesday mornings at 10:00 a.m. and Thursday evenings from 6:30pm there is a club "roll up" to which all members are welcome.

We hold regular free taster sessions for potential members, the only requirement being a pair of flat shoes to help protect our green. If you're interested why not e-mail us here.

Our first taster session will be on Sunday 14th May at 10:00am and as an added bonus we're offering homemade cakes! For full details see here.
