Rob & Freda Bowls Day - 31st August 2015

For the 23rd year the Wilkinson family will be running the annual Rob & Freda two wood triples at Seale & Sands Bowls Club.

All are welcome, members, guests, beginners and bowlers of all abilities will be accommodated.

Trophies will be awarded for the winners and the runners up.

The start time is 10:30am, informal dress but flat shoes please.

Refreshments available and a nominal cost BBQ will be provided.

There is an entrance fee of £2.00 per bowler. All profits will be donated to Seale & Sands Bowls Club.
